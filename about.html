About Creomind Innovations

CreoMind Innovations is a dynamic service-based startup founded in January 2024. Initially focused on EdTech, we have expanded into Web Development, Mobile App Development, and Digital Solutions for institutions and businesses. Our mission is to deliver impactful digital experiences and foster young tech talent through real-time projects and internships.

Our Vision & Mission

To become a leading force in building future-ready tech solutions and nurturing the next generation of skilled professionals in India.


To empower organizations and students with practical, scalable, and innovative digital solutions, while creating an ecosystem of learning and collaboration.

Founder's Message

"At CreoMind Innovations, we believe in growth through innovation and collaboration. What started as a vision to simplify education through technology is now a growing community of creators, problem-solvers, and changemakers. We aim to build not just projects, but people."

Jaganmohan Reddy Bondala, Founder & CEO
